About
KiWi Kisan Window

KiWi KisanWindow is an Organic food Brand based in Dehradun. It promotes organic food products to create a healthy and better lifestyle. KiWi also acts as a window between the farmer and the consumer. Therefore, allowing local farmers to sell their products at better prices and gives the local consumer a chance to buy their organic food, vegetables, fruits, pulses, and many more straight from the farmland.

Organic food brand

KiWi provides a long list of products including fresh and Organic Fruits, Vegetable, Greens, Pulses, Pickles, Spices, Dry Fruits, Tea, Coffee, Milk, Squash and many more. Also, one can buy from the categories based on health-related issues.

Services

There are two stores in Dehradun of KiWi and it also provides ‘delivery at home’ service. By going to the website or KIWI App directly or through other apps like Amazon,Big basket.

Organic farming has a positive impact on the environment. And healthy eating leads to a better lifestyle. With this vision, KiWi works to provide the best organic food brand products for all.
