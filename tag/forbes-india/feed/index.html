Leveraging business tools to make a social impact: Nupur featured in Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia list

As Asia sees the light at the end of the Covid-19 tunnel, activists, entrepreneurs, scientists and young leaders on the Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia 2021 list are hard at work realizing the new normal.

It is a matter of great honour for Nupur to be featured again in Forbes under the 30 under 30 Asia 2021 list for creating a social impact by leveraging KiWi business tools to solve the region’s problem.

“In such difficult times, we just want to be of all help & support to everyone. Only then a great title of Forbes under 30 will be justified. We at KiWi Kisan Window are at your service 24/7 giving you healthy groceries at home with all precautions & hygiene standards.”

– Nupur Agarwal (co-founder of KiWi)

Lending a hand to the farmers: Nupur featured in Forbes India Ultimate 120 people

Innovators in Covid times

A special edition of Forbes India is out on July 2020 in association with Oppo. It profiles entrepreneurs and businesses in the Ultimate 120 with the power to change  by sharing their stories.

It’s a moment of pride for Nupur to get featured as one of the Ultimate 120 for her contribution towards helping the farmers by providing them with seeds & fertilisers during lockdown, while also purchasing produce from them for KiWi, an organic food and wellness brand.

“KKW was always supposed to be an offline retail store, But by March 22, we were taking orders over WhatsApp and delivering to homes. And by April 10, we launched our own website and application.”

– Nupur Agarwal, co-founder of KiWi Kisan Window
