Leveraging business tools to make a social impact: Nupur featured in Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia list

As Asia sees the light at the end of the Covid-19 tunnel, activists, entrepreneurs, scientists and young leaders on the Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia 2021 list are hard at work realizing the new normal.

It is a matter of great honour for Nupur to be featured again in Forbes under the 30 under 30 Asia 2021 list for creating a social impact by leveraging KiWi business tools to solve the region’s problem.

– Nupur Agarwal (co-founder of KiWi)
