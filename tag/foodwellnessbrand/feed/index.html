KiWi in the top ten list of National Startup Awards 2020

ACHIEVEMENT : The results of National Start Up Awards 2020 were announced & it is with immense joy that we inform you, Kiwi Kisan Window bagged a position as a top 10 finalist in the ‘Food‘ category out of almost a lakh startups. We being the only startup from Uttarakhand to have acquired such a position with all categories combined and being recognised by the Government of India is a cherishable achievement. We will keep pushing forward similarly & bringing prestige to Uttarakhand. #NationalStartupAwards2020
